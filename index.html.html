METHODOLOGY & SOURCES

WealthRank uses a composite percentile model combining net worth and income distributions to estimate your position in the global wealth hierarchy. All figures are estimates based on publicly available data.

Wealth Percentile
Based on UBS Global Wealth Report tier distribution (5 tiers, 5.7B adults). Within-tier interpolation uses logarithmic scaling to estimate percentile position.
Income Percentile
Derived from World Bank Poverty & Inequality Platform (PIP) income distribution data. Threshold-based interpolation across 10 income brackets.
Country Ranking
Uses log-normal distribution parameterized by median and mean wealth from UBS Databook. Country income data from ILO and World Bank household surveys.
Generation Ranking
Age-adjusted median wealth estimates from UBS and Federal Reserve SCF data. Log-normal distribution with age-specific sigma reflecting empirical wealth variance.
Salary Data
Profession salary data aggregated from ILO Statistics, Glassdoor Research, LinkedIn Salary Insights, and national statistical offices. Gross annual figures in USD.
Limitations
Data represents estimates and averages. Individual circumstances vary significantly. Figures do not account for cost of living, tax rates, or informal economy income.
